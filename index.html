Featured Projects

Planno

Planno helps bishops of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints create booking links for interviews and meetings, so members can schedule directly from the bishop's availability, reducing coordination time and keeping meetings organized. The scheduling platform allows users to sync their Google Calendars for automatic meeting integration, and members can download meeting information to their Apple Calendars for seamless scheduling across all devices.

TastyFood

TastyFood is a custom food ordering platform that enables local restaurants to accept online orders directly from customers, eliminating the 15-30% commission fees charged by third-party services like DoorDash and UberEats. By owning the ordering system, restaurants keep more revenue per order, maintain direct customer relationships, and have full control over pricing, promotions, and delivery logistics without middleman costs.
